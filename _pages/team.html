Our Members

Erik Boesen

Erik is President of 1418 as well as Programming Captain. Aside from leading development of all our team's software projects, he works on team public relations and with the Virginia House of Delegates to pass legislation aiding FIRST teams in economically disadvantaged communities. He was placed on the FIRST Dean's List in 2018.

Ben Updike

Ben is Mechanics Captain and has been important to our robots' maintenance throughout each competition. Having served initially as our battery manager, he now works on building new features and developing repairs for the robot. During competitions, he serves on our drive team in the role of Technician.
